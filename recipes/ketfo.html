ketfo

description

Ketfo is a traditional Ethiopian dish made from raw minced beef, seasoned with spices and often served with injera. It is known for its rich flavor and is a staple in Ethiopian cuisine.

ingredients

  • 1 lb raw minced beef
  • 2 tablespoons mitmita (Ethiopian spice blend)
  • 1 tablespoon niter kibbeh (spiced clarified butter)
  • Salt to taste
  • Injera (for serving)
  • Fresh greens (optional, for garnish)

instructions

  1. In a bowl, combine the raw minced beef with mitmita and salt. Mix well to ensure the spices are evenly distributed.
  2. In a small pan, gently heat the niter kibbeh until melted, then pour it over the seasoned beef.
  3. Mix everything together until the beef is well coated with the spices and butter.
  4. Serve immediately with injera on the side. Optionally, garnish with fresh greens.
  5. Enjoy your Ketfo with a side of injera, using the bread to scoop up the meat.
